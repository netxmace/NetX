Our Community

We are a community of students passionate about growth through learning, primarily focusing on Free and Open Source Software.
This, therefore, provides a sense of freedom in speech, operations, modification and redistribution of the program. NetX is a learning community of Mar Athanasius College of Engineering, Kothamangalam. Our motto is to "Learn Develop Deploy". We aim at helping just in tech aspects but also in design, documentation, organisation and publicity to name a few.
  • Our Learning Community

    At NetX, we are ardent believer of practical learning through symbiosis.

  • Variety In Scope

    NetX is a fully fledged organinsation which wishes to spread its wings to every department and influence a multitude of students.

  • Family, Our Key To Everything

    Moreover, we are like a family. We work together as a team to emanate the best in each one of us. And ride through our see-saw as a team in equilibrium.
